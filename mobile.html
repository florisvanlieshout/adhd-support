🏠 Family Support Framework

1. Shared Understanding

  • Create shared vocabulary for ADHD vs. choice
  • Monthly education sessions together
  • Document ADHD impacts on household

2. Collaborative Problem-Solving

  • Weekly structured problem-solving meetings
  • Use "we" language, not blame
  • Test solutions for 1-2 weeks

3. Mutual Support

  • Regular needs assessments for both partners
  • Balanced support exchange system
  • Individual self-care requirements

4. Consistent Systems

  • ADHD-friendly visual routines
  • Clear roles and responsibilities
  • Regular system maintenance

5. Celebrating Progress

  • Daily wins recognition
  • Progress celebration rituals
  • Focus on effort, not just outcomes

⚖️ Mental Load Sharing

Why ADHD Brains Struggle

  • Working memory differences
  • Self-regulation issues
  • Tiny distractions cause cracks
  • RSD makes asking for help hard

Solutions for Balance

  • Communication: Speak up clearly, no mind-reading
  • Structure: Weekly family meetings
  • Validation: Acknowledge struggles before requests

💡 Quick Strategies

For Working Memory

  • Voice memos for immediate capture
  • Visual reminders in key locations
  • "Touch it once" rule for paperwork

For Emotional Regulation

  • "Pause button" technique
  • Designated calm-down space
  • Body doubling for tough tasks

For Planning & Organization

  • Time blocking instead of to-do lists
  • Weekly planning with your wife
  • "2-minute rule" - do it now if quick

📈 Progress Tracking

Daily Wins Journal

  • Write 3 things you did well each day
  • Track challenges and how you handled them

Weekly Check-ins

  • Rate your week 1-10 with your wife
  • Review the 5 principles progress

Habit Tracker

  • Simple chart for key routines
  • Celebrate consistency over perfection

🚨 Crisis Management

Immediate De-escalation

  • Recognize: "We're in crisis mode"
  • Time-out: 20-30 minutes apart
  • Regulate: Use calming strategies
  • Reconvene: With structure and appreciation

Emergency Contacts
